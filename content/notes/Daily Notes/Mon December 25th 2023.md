In triangle $OAB$, the $\overrightarrow{OA}$ = 3**i** + 4**k** and the vector $\overrightarrow{OB}$ = **i** + 2**j** - 2**k**. Find $\overrightarrow{OP}$, where $P$ is the point where the bisector of $\angle AOB$ intersects $AB$.
