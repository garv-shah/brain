#physics #kinematics 

$F_{\textrm{on B by A}}=-F_{\textrm{on A by B}}$
$\textrm{Action Force} = -\textrm{Reaction Force}$

For example:

| Action force   | Reaction force          |
| -------------- | ----------------------- |
| Push on planet | Planet pushes on skater |
